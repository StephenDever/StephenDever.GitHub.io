McKenzie Pass: Highway 242

The historic McKenzie highway 242 that connects Eugene to Sisters goes over the Oregon Cascade Mountains. During the winter this road is closed to all motor vehicles due to snow and ice. The McKenzie doesn't open back up until the end of spring, in June sometime. So, for a couple of months in spring the road is both clear of motor vehicles and snow, making it one of the best bike rids on the West Coast.
